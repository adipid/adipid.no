Using [Zint][zint] you can create QR-codes by simply typing the following in
your terminal:

```bash
zint -b QRCODE -d "<data_or_url>" -o <output_filename>.svg
```

You can also swap the SVG-extention with PNG.

[zint]: https://www.zint.org.uk/
